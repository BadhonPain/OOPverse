A Friend function is not a member of a class but still has the access of the private members of the class. 
It's a regular non-member function. (Check General_Use.cpp)
A member of one class can be a friend of another class. (Check General_Use_2.cpp)

Uses:

1.To do Operator Overloading ;    
2.Creation of certain types of I/O functions ;
3.One function to have access to the private members of two or more different class.
